How to Play



Button Purpose
Arrow Keys Used to maneuver your Geometry Ship
Z Fire bullets from the main ship and your Copy Pods
X Activate a screen clearing bomb. A red gauge will show the cooldown timer
Shift Holding shift focuses your Geometry ship for precise movement

Objective
Survive the onslaught of the Evil Squares! It's up to you and your copy pods to take them down.
Killing and dealing damage to Evil Squares will increase your score. Aim for a high score!
