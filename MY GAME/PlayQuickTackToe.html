Welcome to Quick Tac Toe Game

This is a two player game

Rules :

  1. Only drag and drop are allowed
  2. Player can drag their selected image in any empty table cells
  3. One drag and drop is allowed at a time for each player
  4. To win the game, player has to match their images any of the following order :
    • The player who first match their selected three images in a row
    • The player who match first their selected three images in a column
    • The player who first match their selected three images in a diagonal

Click the button to start the game
